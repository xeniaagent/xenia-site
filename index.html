About Xenia

A global perspective, a San Francisco address.

“Guiding you to the right space, while building a relationship that lasts long after the deal is done.”

Born in Serbia and raised in Slovakia, Xenia's journey has spanned Frankfurt, Vienna, and now San Francisco. Fluent in Slovak, German, English, and Serbian, she brings a global perspective to her work.

Her passion for design and architecture — backed by a degree in interior design — began early, fueled by exposure to diverse cultures, and led her to pursue a career in real estate.

Since settling in San Francisco in 2017, Xenia has become intimately familiar with the city's neighborhoods, schools, and family-friendly amenities. Her experience as a mother and homeowner across multiple countries gives her a unique advantage in understanding the diverse needs of her clients, whether buying, selling, or investing.
